Definition of ENVIRONMENTAL BIOLOGY:

Environmental biology defines as:

The scientific study of environment, ecology, living and non-living factors along with their interactions, evolution and global change within a combined form is called "Environmental Biology" or "Ecology".
It examines the ways where organisms, species and communities influence and impacted by natural and human altered ecosystem.
Also, it can be said that; The scientific study of the various relationships exist between the organisms and their environment is called "Ecology".
